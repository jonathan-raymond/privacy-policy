# TIILDE and SuperGrid — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10th 2026

TIILDE/SuperGrid does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party services of any kind in the app.

## What stays on your device

Everything you create in TIILDE/SuperGrid — recorded loops, tuning and layout choices, synth settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your iPad only. It never leaves your device, and we have no access to it. Deleting the app deletes this data.

## Network and MIDI

TIILDE/SuperGrid sends MIDI to instruments you connect (over USB, Bluetooth, or a local network MIDI session you have configured). This is musical performance data only — notes and controller messages — sent to devices you choose, on your own network. TIILDE/SuperGrid makes no other network connections.

## Apple services

If you have opted in to sharing analytics with Apple in your device settings, Apple may provide us with aggregated, anonymized crash reports and usage statistics through App Store Connect. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy, is not linked to your identity, and involves no data collection by TIILDE/SuperGrid itself.

## Children

TIILDE/SuperGrid collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If a future version of TIILDE/SuperGrid ever changes any of the above, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the change will be described in the App Store release notes.
